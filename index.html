I've Some Questions

  • Do I have to be a member of the society to join?

    You don't have to, but we'd love it if you joined us!

  • What can I build?

    Anything you want! We've got a couple of themes to get you going, but don't think you've got to stick to them!

  • I want to join, but I don't think I can code.

    Of course! We're running pre-hack sessions to train everybody up - but if you don't feel like you're up to the task, join a team that needs your skillset!

  • Where is it?

    The Future Technology Center in the University Quarter
